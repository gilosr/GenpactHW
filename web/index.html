Core Metrics
  • Overall Score: 1–5 average based on semantic accuracy.
  • Pass Rate: % of questions scoring 3.0 (Acceptable) or higher.

Scoring Rubric
  • 5 Excellent: 100% accurate facts and numbers.
  • 4 Good: Correct core fact; minor precision variation.
  • 3 Acceptable: Correct subject, but imprecise value.
  • 1-2 Poor/Fail: Wrong facts or off-topic response.
